<objective>
Deduplicate JSDoc documentation between the Monque facade and its internal service methods.

Purpose: The Monque facade (~1294 lines) duplicates extensive JSDoc from service methods. Moving full docs to services and copying them back onto the facade with `@see` cross-references eliminates maintenance overhead while maintaining IDE documentation quality.

**Note:** An `@inheritDoc` approach was originally planned but `@inheritDoc` is not resolved by the TypeScript language server across classes that don't share an inheritance/implementation relationship. Since `Monque` composes its services (not extends them), `@inheritDoc` rendered as literal text in IDE tooltips and was corrected to full JSDoc copy with `@see` references.

Output: Leaner monque.ts with full JSDoc on public methods (copied from service canonical sources) and `@see` cross-references; service files remain the canonical documentation source.
</objective>
